Our Story

Flavors of Phoenicia brings the rich flavors of Lebanon to Ponderay and Sandpoint, Idaho. We serve fresh dips, salads, kabobs, shawarma, desserts, imported drinks, and family-style platters with warm Lebanese hospitality.
